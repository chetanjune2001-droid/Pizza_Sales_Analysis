# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Report

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover key business insights that can help improve revenue, optimize product strategy, and enhance customer experience.

The analysis was performed using **SQL for data extraction and transformation** and **Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization**.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To evaluate sales performance, identify top and bottom-performing products, and provide actionable recommendations to improve overall business efficiency and profitability.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Revenue:** 817.86K  
- **Average Order Value:** 38.31  
- **Total Pizzas Sold:** 49,574  
- **Total Orders:** 21,350  
- **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.32  

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Sales & Order Trends
- Daily order trends to identify peak business days  
- Monthly performance analysis to track growth patterns  

### 🔹 Product Performance
- Top 5 pizzas by revenue, quantity, and orders  
- Bottom 5 pizzas to identify underperforming items  

### 🔹 Category & Size Insights
- Sales contribution by pizza category  
- Sales distribution by pizza size  

---

## 📊 Dashboard

The interactive dashboard (built in Power BI) provides:
- KPI overview  
- Best & worst-selling products  
- Sales trends and distribution  
- Category and size-level insights  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- SQL (Data Analysis & Querying)  
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)  
- Excel (Data Handling)  

---

## 🚀 Key Insights

- A small set of pizzas contributes significantly to overall revenue  
- Certain pizzas consistently underperform across revenue, quantity, and orders  
- Sales show clear daily and monthly patterns indicating demand fluctuations  
- Specific pizza sizes and categories dominate customer preference  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize high-revenue pizzas through targeted promotions and better visibility.  
2. Optimize or remove low-performing items to improve operational efficiency.  
3. Use sales trends to plan inventory, staffing, and marketing campaigns effectively.  
4. Increase average order value through combo deals and upselling strategies.  
5. Focus on high-performing categories and sizes to align with customer demand.  
6. Implement loyalty programs and personalized offers to improve customer retention.
